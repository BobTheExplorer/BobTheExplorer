Climbing this wrought-iron communications tower from when the LIRR (Long Island Rail Road) used to run from here to Long Island City. It also just so happens to be behind the former Coco-Cola Distribution Center, which has since been converted into a shopping outlet.

Right after this, the GoPro managed to fall out of its spot on my head and allllllll the down to the ground, where I couldn't find it until three weeks later when the snow melted.


Surprisingly, it took not even thirty seconds of searching for me to find it; it could have been found or left there by the Chainsaw Man cosplayers doing a photoshoot there. Thankfully I was able to find it and extract what footage I could from the corrupted .mp4 file.
