Down an inconspicuous street surrounded by water are the shuttered ruins of an upscale restaurant. Despite its abandoned status, there is very little to suggest that its windows are still in one piece, and neither has its exterior been covered with thick coats of graffiti. The only characteristic it shares with most abandoned buildings is the years-old chainlink fence that engulfs the former main entrance to the building.Â
