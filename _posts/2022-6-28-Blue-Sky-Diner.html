An unasumming diner on the corner of 49th Ave and 21st St. seems out of the ordinary in the skyscraper-dominated area of Long Island City. Consider it a time capsule of sorts; faux leather booth seating, stainless-steel trimmings, and all the furnishings of a Mountain View Company built diner.
